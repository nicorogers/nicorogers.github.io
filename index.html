Clear

Our Mission

Helping you feel at ease about your finances, so you can focus on living the life you want.

Life is not about earning or saving as much as possible.
It’s about finding the right balance — where money supports the life you want to live, instead of shaping it unconsciously.

For many people, money is a concern.
Not dramatic.
Not urgent.
Just often there.

Clear exists to remove that quiet mental pressure.

We believe that money is a tool.
Not a task.
Not an identity.

The less time you spend on it, the better.

What It Does

Clear is built around simple, real questions people have about their spending — but don’t have clear answers to.

For example:

  • How much of my money actually goes to food?
  • Where can I buy the same products, but cheaper?
  • What is the real cost of owning my house?
  • How much do small, everyday purchases really add up to over a month?
  • How much can I save per month if I stop going to restaurants?

Clear doesn’t overwhelm you with data, spreadsheets, or charts.
It gives clear answers to the questions that matter to you.

One simple question. One simple answer.

Who is it for

Clear is for people who:

  • are tired of spreadsheets, categories, and manual tracking
  • don’t want budgeting or discipline systems
  • want clarity without losing time getting it
  • want money to feel handled, not constantly present

How Clear Works

1. Share your spending

Scan or upload any receipt or invoice, anytime.
The more you scan, the more Clear understands your habits and patterns.

No manual input.
No categorising.

2. Ask Clear a question

You can interact by:

  • asking a simple question in plain language
  • written or by voice

3. Get a simple answer

By voice
Written
Immediately

Over time, Clear becomes more personal — and requires less attention.
